    Products

    Here is a list of products offered by TSS Travelsoft and Services:

    B2B Travel Booking Portal:

    A platform designed for travel agents, agencies and Travel Advisors to access and book various travel services such as flights, hotels, car rentals, and tours for their clients.

    Travel Agency Software:

    Comprehensive software solutions tailored for travel agencies, enabling them to manage bookings, track customer information, generate invoices, and streamline their operations.

    Payment Platform:

    A secure and efficient payment system that facilitates online transactions for travel bookings. This platform enables customers to make payments using various methods, such as credit cards, e-wallets, or bank transfers. You may connect your own payment provider contract or subscribe to our services.

    Payment Providers Services:

    TSS Travelsoft and Services partner with various payment service providers to offer a wide range of payment options to their clients. This includes integrating payment gateways, establishing merchant accounts, and ensuring smooth payment processing.

    B2C Travel Booking Portal:

    A user-friendly website or application that allows individual travelers to search, compare, and book travel services directly, including flights, accommodations, and activities.

    Customer Cancellation Protecting Services:

    A service we provide through a third party that provides protection to customers in case they need to cancel or modify their travel plans. This may include offering refunds, re-booking options, airline bankruptcy or missed connection coverage.

    Fraud Protection Platform:

    A system designed to detect and prevent fraudulent activities related to online travel bookings. It employs advanced algorithms and security measures to safeguard customer information and prevent unauthorized transactions. This is also provided by a third party we partnered to achieve affordable pricing for such seervices.
